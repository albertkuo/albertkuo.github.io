Summer Projects

DIMACS
DIMACS REU 2015
I worked with Dr. Kevin Chen in the REU program at DIMACS (the Center for Discrete Mathematics and Theoretical Computer Science). In my summer project, I analyzed epigenomic data and predicted enhancers using machine learning algorithms. Click here to read more about my project.
SIBS
SIBS 2014
I participated in SIBS (Summer Institute for Training in Biostatistics) at the University of Pittsburgh. For my final project, I worked on the Women's Ischemia Syndrome Evaluation (WISE) study and examined whether persistent chest pain is associated with declines in physical work capacity among women. Read my abstract here.

Side Projects

KNOWVIO
Knowvio (Statistics X)
I am currently working on creating content for the Statistics X app with the Knowvio team. The Statistics X app is designed to be a mobile study tool for introductory college statistics.
METRICS
Econometrics Game
I participated in the 2015 Econometrics Game at the University of Chicago. Our goal was to analyze the effect of obesity on healthcare costs. Our team placed second in the competition and a revised copy of our submitted paper can be found here.
FLAPPY BIRD
Flappy Bird
My friend Anthony and I conducted some statistical modeling and analysis of the mobile game Flappy Bird in 2014. We are currently revisiting the project and will post an updated report soon.
